About Me

Hello, I'm Sophia Laurent

With over a decade behind the lens, I have had the privilege of capturing more than 400 weddings across Malaysia and beyond. I believe every couple has a story that is entirely their own — and it is my calling to preserve those fleeting, luminous moments forever.

My style blends soft natural light, editorial elegance, and candid emotion. From quiet preparations in the bridal suite to the first dance under golden hour — I am there for all of it, unobtrusively and with deep care.

When I am not shooting, you will find me at a florist market, sipping oolong tea, or planning the perfect gallery wall.
